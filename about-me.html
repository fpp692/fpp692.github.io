I am a PhD student in Ecology currently based in Switzerland. I am interested in understanding how species interactions can drive the evolution of species in communities. To do this, I build and use mathematical and computational tools. Outside of science, my hobbies include music, film, photography and learning computing skills.

I've built this site to connect and communicate my various interests in science and beyond. You will find brief summaries of previous scientific projects I have been involved in, photographs I have taken over the years and posts on whatever else I find interesting.

Feel free to explore this site and get in touch.
